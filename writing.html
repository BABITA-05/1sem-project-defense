Have We Met Before?

~Ruby

When days get tough, the heart gets heavier, the body gets tired and life gets devastated. All you wanna do is run away to the person where you can have peace, feel loved and worthwhile.

But you're the person with trust issues, you take time to understand people, to have trust in them. You spend a lot of time, spend years and years with them but still you feel insecure, afraid to share your thoughts and open up yourself to them. Why? Because, spending more time doesn't make you close, connected or understanding to them.

But sometimes it feels so peaceful, so good, so connected to that one person where you've just met few days before. And it made you think, "Have we ever met before?"

By the way, why does it feel like that?
I guess, it is because the way they treat you, the way they make you feel connected, the way they make you feel safe and trustworthy around them. And sometimes, we get more connected to them because of the way of thinking, acting and seeing things with the same perspectives.

Sometimes, you just need that one person where your thoughts can be understood, your heart can be connected, your emotions can be immersed into theirs and your love can be preserved.

If you've already got that person then just keep them safe with you, pour all of your love to them and never let them walk away from your life.
